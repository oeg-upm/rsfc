Research Software Fairness Checks (RSFC)

RSFC is a command-line interface designed to automatically evaluate the quality of a GitHub or GitLab repository.

Authors: Daniel Garijo and Andr√©s Montero

Given a repository URL, RSFC performs a series of checks based on a curated list of Research Software Quality Indicators (RSQI).

RSQI: A research software quality indicator represents a measurable aspect of software, such as FAIRness, test coverage, or documentation completeness.

RSQI are maintained and published by the EVERSE initiative.
